Our Story

For much of the late 19th and 20th century, Stamford was popularly known as the "Lock City", a name owed to Yale & Towne which at its height employed a quarter of the city. While little of Linus Yale's company remains in Stamford today, we continue the same tradition of quality manufacture of reliable products people depend on.

Beginning in 1960 as a division of Accurate Tool and Die, a subcontract manufacturer of specialty locks for Yale & Towne, Accurate Lock & Hardware would later be founded as an independent company by Ronald M. Salvatore. From those humble beginnings, Accurate has taken on orders from specialty high security locks for Attica Prison after the infamous 1971 riots to modest one-off restoration work for historic preservationists.

The Difference

While we are capable of handling large orders, we have never left Stamford. Stamford is our home and we are proud to continue the tradition of quality lock making in our great city. All our products are designed and manufactured locally, on-site. This allows us to precisely control the quality of our products as well as give us unlimited flexibility in customizing them to your needs.

Accurate Lock and Hardware began in 1960 as a division of Accurate Tool and Die manufacturing specialty locks as a sub-contractor for Yale and Towne. In July of 1972, Accurate Lock and Hardware was founded as an independent lock manufacturer. From humble beginnings, the company has evolved into the premier manufacturer of custom architectural hardware products. In the mid-to-late 1970s Accurate developed and furnished institutional locks specifically designed to retrofit existing preparations yet function to meet new code requirements. In fact, in response to the infamous Attica prison riot, Attica officials commissioned Accurate to manufacture custom high security locks. Within the next few years, Accurate introduced a robust dead latch, followed by a narrow backset mortise lock. In the early 1990s we began manufacturing our 9100 series mortise lock which has become the lock of choice among OEM trim manufacturers. Since then, we have expanded our product line to include interior mortise locks, pocket door locks and trim, pivot and Harmon hinges, heavy duty screen door locks, and marine grade locks.
